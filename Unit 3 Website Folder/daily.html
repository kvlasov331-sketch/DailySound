sister

“sister” - The 3rd track on the album boy , which was released in 2024 and thanks to which the masses learned about such a name as 2hollis. An uncomplicated song with an unusual instrumental, at least that's what it seems at first glance. With each listen, you realize that Hollis is not singing about how much he loves a girl, but an unusual sample and previously unused drums and percussion add an inexplicable feeling to this song. Listening to sister from 2hollis, you just want to turn on the noise reduction on your headphones and take a ride through the city at night.

2hollis

(born Hollis Frazier-Herndon on January 5, 2004) is an American artist, producer, and songwriter known for blending electronic music, hip-hop, pop, and experimental sounds. Raised between Chicago and Los Angeles, he began making music at a young age, developing a style that feels both digital and deeply emotional.

He gained wider attention in the early 2020s with projects like White Tiger and later releases that showcased his raw vocals, minimalist production, and introspective themes. 2hollis is often praised for pushing genre boundaries and creating music that reflects internet culture, vulnerability, and youth identity.

Today, he is considered one of the most promising new voices in modern alternative and electronic music, recognized for his distinctive sound and intense live performances.
